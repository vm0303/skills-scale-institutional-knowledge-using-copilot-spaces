# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers are responsible for transforming product requirements into user-friendly, accessible interfaces. They ensure that designs meet usability and accessibility standards and are validated with real users before and during development.

### Responsibilities
- Translate product requirements and user research into wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Ensure designs comply with accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG)
- Collaborate with Product Managers to validate design decisions against user needs
- Partner with Developers during implementation to ensure design fidelity
- Participate in sprint reviews and incorporate feedback iteratively

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and user-validated designs
- Reduce development rework by clarifying design intent early
- Improve user satisfaction and product usability metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with PdM and Developers
- Prototype walkthroughs during sprint planning or kickoff
- Asynchronous design documentation (Figma, design specs, annotation notes)

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works with **Product Manager** to align designs with user needs and acceptance criteria
- Collaborates with **Developers** to clarify design intent and ensure accurate implementation
- Coordinates with **QA/Testing** to validate UI against design specifications
- Presents design decisions to **Stakeholders** for alignment and sign-off

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions. They gather, document, and validate requirements, ensuring that delivery teams have clear, traceable, and agreed-upon scope.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Translate business needs into functional specifications and acceptance criteria
- Maintain requirements traceability from business need to delivery
- Validate that delivered solutions meet defined business requirements
- Support scope management by identifying and documenting change requests

### Goals
- Ensure delivery teams have clear, unambiguous requirements
- Reduce scope creep and rework through early requirements clarity
- Improve alignment between business expectations and technical delivery

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Functional specification documents and user story refinement sessions
- Change request documentation and impact assessments

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works with **Project Manager** to track scope, manage change requests, and update planning artifacts
- Partners with **Product Manager** to refine backlog items and define acceptance criteria
- Supports **Developers** with detailed requirements and clarification during implementation
- Engages **Stakeholders** to validate requirements and obtain sign-off

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers own CI/CD pipelines, deployment automation, and environment reliability. They enable fast, safe, and repeatable delivery by partnering with Developers and QA to streamline releases and support incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Manage environment provisioning, configuration management, and infrastructure as code
- Monitor system health, reliability, and observability (logs, metrics, alerts)
- Support rollback and incident response protocols
- Collaborate on release readiness and pre-release gate validation

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize deployment risk through automation and observability
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness syncs with PM, Developers, and QA
- Incident response coordination and post-incident retrospectives
- Infrastructure and pipeline documentation updates

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Partners with **Developers** to integrate CI/CD best practices into the development workflow
- Coordinates with **QA/Testing** for automated test integration and release smoke tests
- Works with **Project Manager** to schedule deployment windows and confirm rollback plans
- Supports **Stakeholders** by ensuring environment stability and availability during releases

---

## Data Analyst / Data Scientist

### Role Summary
Data Analysts and Data Scientists support product and project teams by defining metrics, performing analyses, and translating data insights into actionable recommendations. They enable data-informed decision making throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Define and instrument KPIs and success metrics in collaboration with Product Managers
- Analyze product usage, experiment results, and operational data
- Build dashboards and reports to surface key signals for the team
- Support A/B testing and experiment design
- Contribute data-driven insights to retrospectives and continuous improvement cycles

### Goals
- Enable data-informed prioritization and decision making
- Ensure success metrics are measurable, tracked, and acted upon
- Surface insights that drive product and process improvement

### Typical Communication
- Metrics reviews and experiment readout sessions with PdM and PM
- Dashboard documentation and data dictionary maintenance
- Retrospective contributions with data-backed observations

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Collaborates with **Product Manager** to define and measure success metrics and run experiments
- Works with **Project Manager** to incorporate metric reviews into reporting cadences
- Partners with **Developers** to instrument features and ensure data collection accuracy
- Presents insights to **Stakeholders** during monthly updates and milestone reviews
